1. User Authentication
As a user, I want to sign in with my credentials so I can access the app.
As a user, I want to log out securely.


2. Admin stories

As an admin, I want to edit or remove doctors and nurses.
As a admin, I want to see all statistics at clinic, doctors, nurses, patients.
As an admin, I want to see statistics over a chosen period (daily, weekly, monthly).

3. Nurse stories

As a nurse, I want to update patient information if it changes.
As a nurse, I want to cancel or reschedule appointments.
As a nurse, I want to see the schedule of doctors for better planning.
As a nurse, I want to filter patients by condition, doctor, or appointment date.
 
4. Doctor stories
As a doctor, I want to see patient appointment and report history
As a doctor, I want to see my all patients.
As a doctor, I want to have nice page for writing reports.
As a doctor, I want to edit or finalize patient reports.
